Academic identity

Asociația Grupul Verde delivers structured IT training through programs developed in the Cisco Networking Academy ecosystem.

The academy combines curriculum rigor with lab practice and skill-based evaluation. The training model is built for two concrete outcomes: professional preparation of learners and increased access to technology education in the community.

Asociația Grupul Verde in the role of Cisco Academy

Through its developed IT training programs, Asociația Grupul Verde functions as an academy within the Cisco Networking Academy ecosystem, with local implementation of relevant modules. The institutional model follows curriculum rigor, sustained practice, and standardized evaluation.

  • Curriculum: structured by modules and levels, with explicit academic objectives.
  • Instructors: technical mentoring and guidance focused on measurable outcomes.
  • Evaluation: transparent criteria, monitored progress, and personalized recommendations.

The social-educational component of the association

The mission of Asociația Grupul Verde complements academic training through digital inclusion initiatives, career guidance, and expanded access to technology education for youth and adults. IT training is treated as long-term local development infrastructure.

  • Digital initiation workshops for communities with limited access to training.
  • Academic and professional guidance sessions for realistic IT paths.
  • Educational projects with social, technological, and civic responsibility themes.
